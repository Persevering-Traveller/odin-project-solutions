Cajun Blackened Catfish

Description

A delicious Cajun style of cooking Catfish with cajun spices and a flavorful charred crust!

Ingredients

  • 1tsp of Kosher Salt
  • 1tsp of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1tsp of Ground Cayenne Pepper
  • 1tsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1tsp of Onion Powder
  • 1tsp of Paprika
  • 1tsp of Dried Parsley
  • 1/2tsp of Dried Oregano
  • 1/2tsp of Dried Thyme
  • 4 4oz Catfish Fillets, skinned
  • 3/4 cup of unsalted butter
