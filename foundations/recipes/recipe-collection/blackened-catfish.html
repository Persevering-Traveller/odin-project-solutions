Cajun Blackened Catfish

Image owned by Hank Shaw and Holly A. Heyser

Description

A delicious Cajun style of cooking Catfish with cajun spices and a flavorful charred crust!

Ingredients

  • 1tsp of Kosher Salt
  • 1tsp of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1tsp of Ground Cayenne Pepper
  • 1tsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1tsp of Onion Powder
  • 1tsp of Paprika
  • 1tsp of Dried Parsley
  • 1/2tsp of Dried Oregano
  • 1/2tsp of Dried Thyme
  • 4 4oz Catfish Fillets, skinned
  • 3/4 cup of unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Mix together Salt, Black Pepper, Cayenne Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Paprika, Parsley, Oregano, and Thyme in a bowl until thoroughly combined.
  2. Press Catfish Fillets into the spice mixture to coat.
  3. Melt Butter in a bowl and set aside.
  4. Ready your grill (you can use a butane burner or a side burner on your grill). Light your burner and place a large cast-iron skillet onto the burner over a High Heat. Pour ~1/4 cup of melted butter into skillet.
  5. When butter begins smoking, lay catfish fillets into the skillet and cook until the spices are burned onto the fillets and are opaque and flaky inside (~3 minutes per side).
  6. Once catfish are cooked, pour the remaining 1/2 cup of butter over the catfish and enjoy!!
