Stovetop Cheese Enchiladas

Description

A quick and easy recipe for cheese enchiladas on the stovetop! Done in 30 minutes, and can easily use the cans of ingredients as measuring cups!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Hamburger
  • 16oz of Fiesta Blend Shredded Cheese
  • 1 can of 10oz Mild Red Enchiladas Sauce
  • 1 can of 10.5oz Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 3/4 cup (in enchiladas sauce can) of Milk
  • 1 can of 4oz Diced Green Chiles or ~10 slices of Jalapenos, chopped
  • Half an Onion, diced
  • 18 Small Corn or Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Brown and chop up hamburger in Medium Heat skillet with diced onions.
  2. Once hamburger is browned, add Enchilada Sauce, Cream of Mushroom, Green Chiles/Jalapenos, and Milk. Mix together thoroughly.
  3. Once mixed, place Corn/Flour Tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave for ~1 minute.
  4. Then pinch as much cheese as you would like in each toritilla (be sure to leave enough for every enchilada!!) and lay it in a line in the middle of the tortilla. Then roll the cheese in the tortilla.
  5. Place the rolled tortilla in the skillet, lip of of the roll facing down towards the skillet, starting from the top left side. Place each additional rolled tortilla next to it until you reach the other edge of the skillet, then create another line of tortillas.
  6. Once all tortillas have been placed, sprinkle any leftover cheese over the tortillas, and let cook until the cheese ontop has been melted.
  7. Then turn off skillet, set aside for ~3 minutes, serve, and enjoy!
