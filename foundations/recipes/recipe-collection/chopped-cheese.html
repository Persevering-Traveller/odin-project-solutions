Chopped Cheese

Description

A type of sandwich originating from New York City. Made of grilled groundbeef, onions, peppers, melted cheese, and condiments. Somewhat similar to a Sloppy Joe, and very delicious!!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Hamburger
  • Montreal Seasoning or Cracked Pepper and Salt(to taste)
  • 10-15 slices of Banana Peppers or Jalapenos
  • 8 slices of American Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Onion
  • 1/2 Cup of Diced Tomatoes
  • ~1Tbsp of Mayo
  • ~1Tbsp of Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cook and break up hamburger in medium heat skillet.
  2. Add the seasoning and peppers, and let them cook through the still cooking hamburger.
  3. Once hamburger is browned, fold in the cheese, onion, tomatoes, and condiments.
  4. Once thoroughly mixed, turn off skillet when the cheese is melted.
  5. Place chopped cheese mix into your bun of choice and enjoy!
